idiosyncratic tags

  • bit: - Informal. An amusement, a funny thing to say, a bit of business. Sometimes a flight of fantasy, a stretched metaphor or an attempt to make a point vividly. e.g. tomb_of_the_mistaken_scientist, theories_as_suits_of_armour.
  • undisputed: an established fact, or definition worth remembering. A key definition, example or known position oft used bit of jargon for which it is good to know the precise details or have a canonical account. An established result NOT controversial or the subject of (widespread) dispute. e.g. extensional, gettier_counterexamples.
  • idea: a passing idea to remember for later. Maybe sketchy, fragmentary or merely suggestive. May be developed into something more later.
  • topic: a broad issue. may be a hub linking to many other cards
  • thesis: a key claim in my overall view.
