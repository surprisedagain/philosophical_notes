extensional

short

The extension of a predicate is the class of objects of which it is true. The term extension is commonly used(/extended) to apply to names and relationships as well. The extension of a name is the object it names/refers to. The extension of a relationship is the class of tuples of objects of which it is true.

An occurrence of a term (that has an extension) is extensional iff every replacement of that term with another that has the same extension (i.e. is true of the same tuples of objects) produces a sentence whose truth value is the same as the original.

extensionalism

The disputed doctrine that

  • the language required for science,
  • a complete description of the world,
  • the language is which the world may be completely described OR
  • ordinary language

is purely extensional (i.e. all grammatically correct occurrences of terms are extensional).

connection to truth functional connectives

The notion of extensional terms/contexts/sentences has a straightforward connection to truth functional connectives.

In a extensional context the extension of a predicate(/relationship) and the object referred to by the term to which that predicate is applied determines the truth of the containing sentence(/sentential phrase).
